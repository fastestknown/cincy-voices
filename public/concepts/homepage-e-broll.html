What is Fractional Leadership?

Fractional leaders are seasoned executives who work with multiple companies instead of one. They bring C-suite experience — CMO, COO, CFO, CTO — at a fraction of the cost and commitment of a full-time hire.

For growing companies, it means getting the strategic leadership you need without the overhead you can't afford. For the leaders themselves, it means building a career around variety, autonomy, and impact.

Cincinnati's fractional community is unusually tight. These leaders refer each other, collaborate on projects, and genuinely root for each other's success. That's what makes this city different — and that's what Cincy Voices set out to capture.
