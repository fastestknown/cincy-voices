The Idea

We recorded the conversations that matter most.

Cincinnati's best business thinking happens in private — in conference rooms, over coffee, between sessions. We asked: what if we could capture those moments and make them available to everyone?

So we brought 17 leaders into a room, turned on the cameras, and let them talk.

The Method

Not interviews. Conversations.

01

Curate

Hand-picked 17 leaders whose perspectives represent the breadth and depth of Cincinnati's business community.

02

Facilitate

Created a space for genuine dialogue — no scripts, no talking points, no agenda beyond honest exchange.

03

Distill

From hours of footage, we extracted 182 clips and 65 quotes — the moments that capture the essence of each voice.
