How We Captured
Cincinnati's Voice

A behind-the-scenes look at how Cincy Voices went from an idea to a living archive of the city's most thoughtful business leaders.

The Process

From concept to conversation to code

Phase 01
Identify the Voices
Curated 17 Cincinnati business leaders across industries — people whose perspectives deserved a bigger stage.
Phase 02
Facilitate the Conversations
Brought leaders together in small groups for unscripted dialogue. No talking points. No agenda. Just real conversation.
Phase 03
Capture Everything
Multi-camera video, full transcription, and careful curation. 182 clips and 65 quotes distilled from hours of footage.
Phase 04
Thread the Insights
Organized perspectives into 7 topic threads — weaving different voices together around shared themes.
Phase 05
Build the Experience
Designed and built an immersive platform that makes you feel like you're in the room, hearing it firsthand.
