# Adam Reynolds — Leader Profile Reference

## Role & Identity
**Fractional CFO** | Founder & CEO of Thrive CFO
Specializes in turnaround transformations — his sweet spot is businesses 6-7 months from going under, which he takes to record profits. CFO-first, full-stack financial leadership for companies that need more than bookkeeping.

---

## The Origin Story

Adam started as an intern and, together with his mentor, built a fractional CFO offering before "fractional CFO" was even a term. They just knew that using data to drive growth was more powerful than weekly bookkeeping.

> "Before fractional CFO work was even a thing, we just knew that using data to help grow, scale, forecast, predict growth, and plan for a better future was more powerful than just getting your books worked on each week."

He was on track to become partner at his former firm, but had fundamentally different visions for how to work and take care of the team. He held himself to standards that didn't align with where the firm was headed — so he left to start Thrive CFO.

> "I hold myself to certain standards of how to take care of the team, how to take care of the clients, how to deliver work."

---

## What Makes Adam Remarkable

### 1. Pioneer of the Fractional CFO Model
Adam was doing fractional CFO work before the term existed. While the industry has since caught up to the concept, he was already proving the model — using data to help businesses grow, scale, and plan for a better future.

### 2. Turnaround Specialist with Life-Changing Results
His sweet spot is companies on the brink. He doesn't just save businesses — he transforms the lives of everyone in them. Owners, employees, families.

> "We didn't just change the owner's lives, we changed everybody's lives."

**Case studies:**
- **Professional services firm:** Took a company losing $1M per year and turned it into $500-600K in profit
- **General contractor:** Grew from $8M to $55M in revenue
- **Entertainment company:** Flipped a $250K loss to $150K profit and expanded from 2 to 7 locations

### 3. The Planning Workshop — Focus as Superpower
Adam uses a proprietary planning workshop: 100-150 brainstormed growth ideas filtered down to 1-3 high-impact actions. In a world of infinite options, focus is everything.

> "There's always a million different ways you can grow and scale. Focusing on the top ones can really get you there faster."

### 4. CFO-First, Full-Stack Model
He doesn't try to be a little bit of everything for a lot of people. He goes deep with the right clients and becomes their entire financial leadership layer.

> "I want to be everything for the right person. I don't want to be anything for anyone."

### 5. People-First AND Profitable
Adam rejects the false choice between taking care of your team and being profitable. He's found the path that does both — better pay, better benefits, 401k — funded by better financial performance.

> "A lot of people sacrifice their profits for taking care of their team. And there's a way to do both. And I love sharing that path with those business owners."

## Philosophy & Approach

- **Data over gut:** Every decision backed by numbers, not instinct
- **Gross profit is king:** Revenue is vanity — gross profit is the number that matters
- **Focus is everything:** Out of 100 possible growth levers, find the 1-3 that move the needle
- **People AND profit:** You can take care of your team and be profitable — it's not either/or
- **Exit planning matters:** 90+ percent of business owners need their business to fund retirement, and most businesses aren't worth enough yet

> "Gross profit's your most important number."

> "90 plus percent of business owners need a value to live their personal life comfortably when they retire, and their business isn't worth that."
