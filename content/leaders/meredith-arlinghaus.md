# Meredith Arlinghaus — Leader Profile Reference

## Role & Identity
**Fractional Accounting Services** | Financial Clarity for Small Business Owners
Provides fractional accounting and financial management for small businesses, specializing in cash flow, transparency, and helping owners see their numbers clearly — both rearview and forward-looking.

---

## The Origin Story

### Small Town Entrepreneurship
Meredith grew up in a small town in northwest Ohio — the kind with no chain stores, where everybody owns something. Her parents, despite having corporate careers, bought the local ice cream shop. Meredith and her sisters worked there every summer.

> "I'm from a really small town in northwest Ohio. It's one of those towns that has no chains. Everybody owns something. My parents owned the ice cream shop. It just kind of set that entrepreneurial seed that everybody just owns a business."

Her father was the biggest influence — a sales executive who also ran the ice cream shop as a side hustle while raising a family and giving back to the community.

### The Breaking Point
Meredith walked away from a six-figure salary as the primary breadwinner in her household. It wasn't a career move — it was survival.

> "I literally hit that breaking point in my corporate career where I knew something was going to break. Our family or me personally. And I knew I had to step away from that position to save myself and my family."

### A Mom Who Built Her Own Job
With a 4-year-old, a 2-year-old, and a newborn, Meredith couldn't find a job that let her be both a working professional and the mom she wanted to be. So she created one.

> "I wanted to be there for them to help with their homework, to pick them up from school, to coach their teams. That's what I wanted to do, but I also wanted to continue working. So I had to find and actually create my own job to be able to do that for my family."

---

## What Makes Meredith Remarkable

### 1. She Watches Shoulders Drop
Meredith's superpower is watching the physical transformation that happens when a small business owner finally gets financial clarity. You can literally see the burden lift.

> "To talk to them and literally see their shoulders relax once you come in and start working with them, you can see the burden absolutely being lifted off of them. And that is probably one of the most rewarding parts of my job."

### 2. The Payroll Hug
One of her most visceral stories: a client who couldn't make payroll. Literally losing sleep over it. Meredith's team came in, helped them understand their expense base, freed up cash. The client hugged her.

> "I've literally been hugged by a client who was able to make payroll for the first time in a month. They were literally losing sleep because they did not have the cash in the bank."

### 3. She Demands Transparency
Meredith learned that you can't help someone who won't open their books. One prospective client straight-up said they couldn't show all their bank accounts because personal finances were mixed in. Meredith had to walk away.

> "If you cannot, you're not willing to open your books — unfortunately, we cannot help you unless you're willing to lay it all out there. You have to let us in to some of the most intimate parts of your business."

### 4. She Calls Small Business Owners Culture Shifters
Meredith doesn't just serve small business owners — she reveres them.

> "I have been around entrepreneurs and small business owners my entire life, and I do think they are culture shifters. They're the backbone of our communities."

## Philosophy & Approach

- **Rearview + forward view:** Doesn't just look at actuals (what happened) but helps clients see where they're headed
- **Fiduciary responsibility:** Trust is the foundation — clients must believe she's working in their best interest
- **Immediate value demonstration:** Can tie a direct correlation between her services and the value provided, because financial clarity drives immediate actionable decisions
- **Bet on yourself:** Walking away from corporate taught her she could build something on her own terms
- **Fractional is exploding:** Sees adoption doubling every 2 years, projects massive growth in 5-10 years, driven by post-COVID flexibility needs
